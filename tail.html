Facts about a Cat's Tails


Cats are the only felines that can hold their tails up

  • Their tails help them balance

  • Their tails are connected to their spine
    Some cats will seem un bothered in you step on their tail. In reality, they can feel it and it could possilbly cause them spinal problems.

  • Cats use their tails to communicate

  • Cats are able to live without their tails
